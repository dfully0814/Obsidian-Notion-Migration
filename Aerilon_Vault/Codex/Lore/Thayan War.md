# Description
Brooding tensions between the nations of [[Aerilon]] and [[Thay]] have erupted into all out war.

The major known front of the war is currently raging in the large wasteland area of the [[Anauroch Desert]] lead by [[General Thrul Kren]] from the Thayan army.
